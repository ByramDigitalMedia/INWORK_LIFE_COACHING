About Me

Who is Savant Crockett? I’m a certified life coach with over 10 years of leadership and mentoring experience-guiding men and young people through emotional struggles, life transitions and personal growth. My background includes business studies at Rutgers University and Boston College, plus years spent working with youth at the Boys & Girls Club as the Director of Teen Services, mentoring children with autism through fitness and leading men’s wellness and self-defense groups. My mission is simple: to help men become whole-from the inside out.

InWork Life Coaching

I coach men who are ready to step into a better version of yourself. My services focus on:

  • Finding stability, peace, and power without losing your edge
  • Whether you’re expecting a child, adjusting to fatherhood or just trying to figure it all out
  • Reclaiming your life and rewriting your story
  • Building calmness, clarity, and self-control through grounded practices

This is coaching for the man who wants more—for himself, his family, and his future.

If you are ENGAGED in the Coaching Process, I can promise you a TRANSFORMATIONAL EXPERIENCE.

The first job of a Coach is to IDENTIFY your STRENGTHS and PASSIONS! What do you do WELL? What do you LIKE AND LOVE to do? What particular aspects of your personality serve to ENHANCE your life?

Why talk to a COACH? Here are some reasons:

  • You want to gain a better UNDERSTANDING of where you are in life and how this affects your mind and soul. You want EDUCATION about your EXECUTIVE FUNCTIONS.
  • You want to FEEL good about yourself instead of constantly feeling like you are not MEETING your POTENTIAL as a student or in your career and in your personal life. You want to stop feeling like an UNDERACHIEVER ("If only you would try harder!!").
  • You want to MANAGE (and ideally EMBRACE) your situation so that it does not MANAGE YOU in ways that you do not like.
  • But MOSTLY you want USABLE and REPEATABLE STRATEGIES and SOLUTIONS to your often CHRONIC CHALLENGES which typically revolve around:
    • Organization
    • Time management
    • Procrastination
    • Relationships
    • Controlling Impulsiveness
    • Focus and Sustained Concentration
